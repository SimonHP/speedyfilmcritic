About Movie Score Aggregator

Movie Score Aggregator was created to solve a simple problem: movie fans often need to visit multiple sites — IMDb, Rotten Tomatoes, Metacritic, and TMDb — to get a sense of how a film is received. Our app brings these ratings together instantly in one place.

What Makes This Different?

  • Unified Ratings: We calculate an easy-to-read average from all the major review sites.
  • Streaming Availability: Instantly see where you can legally stream the film in the UK.
  • Real-Time Data: Results are pulled live from trusted APIs, so scores are always up to date.
  • User-Friendly: No ads, no clutter — just search and get the information you need.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this official?

No. We use public APIs and present ratings side by side for convenience.

Is it free?

Yes. Our goal is to make film discovery and comparison faster for everyone.
